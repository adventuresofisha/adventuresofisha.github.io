One of the greatest joys in life is to travel...

Currently based in Seattle, traveling within the USA and internationally.

"To travel is to take a journey into yourself".

Hi, my name is Isha! I grew up fortunate enough to travel often and went around the world quite a bit, and as I've entered my 20s I have started to travel on my own more and more. Traveling is extremely valuable, both for personal growth and happiness. Exploring the world leads to more adventures, an open mind, and plenty of stories to tell from your unique experiences. And just as important as adventure is your safety - so I'll give tips and guidance for you to make your trips more safe, so you can relax and have fun exploring our planet. Here, my goal is to give international travel advice and some tips for traveling safely based on your destination, especially for young women like myself, hoping to inspire, reassure, and motivate you to start your own journey of traveling. Cheers to more adventures! ~ Isha, 21
