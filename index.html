Adventures of Isha

One of the greatest joys in life is to travel...

"To travel is to take a journey into yourself".

Hi, my name is Isha! I grew up fortunate enough to travel often and went around the world quite a bit, and as I've entered my 20s I have started to travel on my own more and more. Traveling is extremely valuable, both for personal growth and happiness. Exploring the world leads to more adventures, an open mind, and plenty of stories to tell from the journey. Here, I'll give international travel advice and some tips for traveling safely, especially for young women like myself. Cheers to more adventures! ~ Isha, 21
