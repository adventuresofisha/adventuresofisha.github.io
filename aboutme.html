MY LIFE

I was born and raised in Seattle, Washington in the Pacific Northwest part of the United States of America. Seattle and the surrounding areas in Washington are beautiful, and contain so much to discover in the outdoors, from various mountain ranges, to the Pacific Ocean and Puget Sound, to countless rivers and Alpine lakes, to a desert in eastern Washington that is a totally different climate from western Washington. I feel fortunate to have grown up here, and it will always be a special state to me. My extended family is quite spread out around the world, and my parents love to travel too, so I have already been to a lot of cool places in the world and across 4 continents. Now, I am 21 years old and a current undergraduate college student in Seattle, studying computer science. I have continued to travel around the world with my family, friends, through academic and career competitions, and sometimes by myself. Now that I've started to travel more without my parents and family there, my friends and I have started to truly understand how important safety is while traveling in a new place. And so I want to inspire other people, especially young women like myself, to feel prepared and safe while traveling!

MY TRAVELS

Although I grew up in the United States and have spent the majority of my life here, I've always been an international traveler and drawn to experiencing other countries, cultures, foods, sights, and traditions. My first international trip was to Amsterdam, in the Netherlands, when I was less than a year old! Since then, I've been to 29 of the 50 US states, 17 different countries, 4 of the 7 continents, and 3 of the 7 wonders of the world. My goal is to eventually visit all 7 continents, all 7 wonders of the world, and as many countries as I can!

My Bucket List (Next) Destinations:
